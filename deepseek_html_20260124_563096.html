Who We Are

Blak Diamond Social Care Ltd is a specialist social care provider focused on supporting young people aged 8–18, especially those transitioning through challenging circumstances toward semi-independent living.

Our Mission

To foster growth, resilience and self-sufficiency by delivering tailored, reliable care and accommodation solutions.

Our Philosophy

We operate with a youth-centred philosophy, blending clinical expertise with lived experience-informed practices to guide individuals toward meaningful independence.

Our Team

Led by experienced social workers and care professionals with strong backgrounds in trauma, behavioural support and multi-agency collaboration.

What We Do

Blak Diamond delivers a comprehensive social care ecosystem spanning housing, support and clinical guidance for vulnerable young people.

Supported Living Accommodation

Safe, structured housing options that combine residential care with skills-based support. Environments are designed for stability, learning and emotional growth.

Solo & High-Support Placements

Individualised placements for young people with significant behavioural or emotional needs—especially those transitioning from secure settings or under court orders.

Personalised Care & Domiciliary Support

Care services adjusted to meet complex needs, delivered in both residential and home-based settings to promote comfort and continuity.

Behavioural Intervention & Developmental Support

Specialised strategies to manage challenging behaviours while building life skills, social functioning and personal responsibility.

Staff Training & Development Programs

Continuous professional development ensures our staff deliver care that is safe, evidence-led, responsive and compassionate.

Referral & Transition Support

Collaborative work with local authorities, families, educators and health professionals to create smooth transitional pathways out of care.

Our Approach

At Blak Diamond, every young person's support plan is individualised, culturally sensitive and outcome-focused.

  • Strength-based planning
  • Holistic wellbeing support
  • Clear progression goals
  • Safety, dignity and respect
  • Skill building toward independence

We reject one-size-fits-all care. Instead, we invest in meaningful relationships that open doors to future opportunities.

Why We Matter

Young people in care often face disrupted education, complex trauma and limited opportunities. Blak Diamond's services are designed to:

Stabilise Living Situations

Providing consistent, safe housing environments that offer stability and routine.

Improve Emotional Regulation

Helping young people develop confidence and emotional management skills.

Equip with Life Skills

Teaching practical skills needed for independent living and future success.

Reduce High-Risk Settings

Minimizing re-entry into higher-risk environments through supportive transitions.

We work closely with commissioners, social workers, families and all stakeholders to improve outcomes every step of the way.
