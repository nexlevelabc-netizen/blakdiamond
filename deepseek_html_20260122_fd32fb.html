Who We Are

Blak Diamond Social Care Ltd is a specialist social care provider focused on supporting young people aged 8–18, especially those transitioning through challenging circumstances toward semi-independent living. Our mission is to foster growth, resilience and self-sufficiency by delivering tailored, reliable care and accommodation solutions.

We operate with a youth-centred philosophy, blending clinical expertise with lived experience-informed practices to guide individuals toward meaningful independence. Our team is led by experienced social workers and care professionals with strong backgrounds in trauma, behavioural support and multi-agency collaboration.

What We Do

Supported Living Accommodation

Safe, structured housing options that combine residential care with skills-based support. Environments are designed for stability, learning and emotional growth.

Solo & High-Support Placements

Individualised placements for young people with significant behavioural or emotional needs—especially those transitioning from secure settings or under court orders.

Personalised Care & Domiciliary Support

Care services adjusted to meet complex needs, delivered in both residential and home-based settings to promote comfort and continuity.

Behavioural Intervention & Developmental Support

Specialised strategies to manage challenging behaviours while building life skills, social functioning and personal responsibility.

Staff Training & Development Programs

Continuous professional development ensures our staff deliver care that is safe, evidence-led, responsive and compassionate.

Referral & Transition Support

Collaborative work with local authorities, families, educators and health professionals to create smooth transitional pathways out of care.

Our Approach

Strength-based planning

We focus on building upon each young person's unique strengths and capabilities.

Holistic wellbeing support

Addressing physical, emotional, social, and educational needs for comprehensive care.

Clear progression goals

Establishing measurable milestones to track development toward independence.

Safety, dignity and respect

Creating environments where young people feel secure, valued, and heard.

Skill building toward independence

Practical training in life skills essential for successful independent living.

We reject one-size-fits-all care. Instead, we invest in meaningful relationships that open doors to future opportunities.

Why We Matter

Young people in care often face disrupted education, complex trauma and limited opportunities. Blak Diamond's services are designed to make a meaningful difference.

Stabilise living situations

Providing consistent, safe environments for vulnerable young people.

Improve emotional regulation

Helping young people develop coping strategies and build confidence.

Equip with life skills

Teaching essential skills for independent living and personal responsibility.

Reduce high-risk re-entry

Decreasing the likelihood of returning to higher-risk environments.

We work closely with commissioners, social workers, families and all stakeholders to improve outcomes every step of the way.
